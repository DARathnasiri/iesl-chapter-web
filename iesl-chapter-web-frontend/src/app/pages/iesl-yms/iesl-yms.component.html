IESL YMS

What is YMS?
The YMS (Young Members Section) act as the link between young engineers and IESL. It helps to make the youth in the field of engineering more attracted to IESL and inform the board of young members (members, associate members, and student members) of the relevant affairs and activities. Their plans and work priorities function as the interface for all young members to stand out in their careers as outstanding engineers and provide a stage for their voices.

Projects under IESL-YMS

Talk to School Program

As part of the Social Responsibility Program, the IESL YMS launched an awareness program called "Talk to School" to encourage school-age children to choose engineering as their career. The program aims to educate students on how to apply mathematics and physics subjects creatively and usefully, thereby instilling students in their advanced education to learn mathematics courses. These plans will be carried out with the support of the organization, implementation, and resource search of the provincial branch.

Talk to University Program

YMS plans to run the "Talk to University" program. The main goal of the program is to enrich students with the correct attitude expected by the industry. This program makes the students aware of new trends, challenges and career opportunities in the industry.

Field Visits and Meet an Engineer Program

These projects will fill the gap between university education and industry expectations. The program will be led by senior and junior engineers in the industry, who will share their experiences to motivate recent graduates to acquire the necessary technical and social skills required by the industry. This will encourage recent graduates to participate in IESL activities and obtain associate membership.

Young Journal and National Engineering Conference

This provides a good opportunity to publish the research results of YMS members. In order to make the IESL National Engineering Conference the primary event for the engineering community to issue policy statements and project proposals related to the country's achievement of sustainable development goals, YMS is willing to actively participate in these plans.

Inter-University English Debate

Interuniversity Debate Competition 2021 is to allow students to think critically while improving communication skills. They will analytically evaluate the opinions of others and logically construct their refutations. They should express their ideas in a clear professional style, which will enable them to develop their speaking skills. In addition, they will find that this is an opportunity to study and learn the attributes of expert debaters, and to instil these attributes into themselves. It is not limited to maintaining proper body language, keeping calm during disagreements, sticking to the problem at hand, and speaking out loud. Speak clearly and deal with emotions. It will help students build confidence and improve their interpersonal skills. Ultimately, this event will build a bridge between universities, promote communication and networking within the university, and ultimately benefit all graduates entering the industry.

IESL YMS Blog

The IESL YMS blog aims to provide a valuable space for young Sri Lankan engineers, passionate about creativity, who share their new and innovative ideas and inspiration to contribute to the betterment of the country and society as a whole.
